Hello there. I'm Sparsh.

I’ve always had this strong urge to tell stories — even though, honestly, my writing skills are… well, not great. But I’m trying. And that’s what matters.

If you’re reading this, it means you clicked a random link on the internet and somehow landed here — on a website that’s actually live and visible to anyone in the world. Kinda wild, right?

I come from a commerce background, and I put this whole thing together overnight. No rocket science, just curiosity and a little courage. Still, I’m proud I tried.

So yeah… I’ll be writing things here. Some might be messy. Some might be honest. Some might even be good. I just hope someone out there enjoys them.

Let’s go.
